About Autopilot Automation

Combining 9+ years of QA automation experience with a focus on practical, maintainable solutions.

Autopilot Automation was created to help teams ship software with confidence. Instead of chasing shiny tools, we focus on frameworks that your team can maintain, extend, and trust release after release.

Led by a Senior QA Automation Engineer

Behind Autopilot Automation is a Senior Software Test Automation Engineer with over 9 years of experience in IT and Software Quality Assurance. Over the years, I have built and maintained automation frameworks for web, mobile, and API testing across multiple industries and tech stacks.

I understand both the technical details and the business realities: limited time, limited resources, and high expectations from users and stakeholders.

  • 9+ years in QA automation and testing
  • Experience with Selenium, Playwright, Cypress, Appium, RestAssured
  • Hands-on work with CI/CD, BrowserStack, and cloud testing
  • Strong focus on reporting, stability, and maintainability
